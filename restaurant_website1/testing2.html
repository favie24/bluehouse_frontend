Creamy Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti (250g)
  • Minced beef (200g)
  • Tomato paste (3 tbsp)
  • Onion (1, chopped)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Salt and seasoning to taste

Recipe:

Boil the spaghetti until soft and set aside. In a pan, heat olive oil, saut√© onions and garlic, then add minced beef and cook until brown. Stir in tomato paste, seasoning, and a little water. Allow to simmer, then mix in the spaghetti. Serve hot with grated cheese.

Spicy Grilled Fish

Ingredients:

  • Fresh tilapia or catfish (1 medium)
  • Vegetable oil (2 tbsp)
  • Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Pepper, salt, and paprika (to taste)
  • Lemon juice (1 tbsp)

Recipe:

Clean the fish and marinate with oil, lemon juice, garlic, pepper, and spices. Allow to rest for 30 minutes. Grill on low heat until both sides are golden and well cooked. Serve with pepper sauce or plantains.

Recipe:
